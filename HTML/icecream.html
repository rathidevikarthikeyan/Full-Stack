Ice cream

Ice cream is a frozen dessert typically made from milk or cream that has been flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice, suascocoa or vanilla, or with fruit, such as strawberries or peaches.
Food colouring is sometimes added in addition to stabilizers.
